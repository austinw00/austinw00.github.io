Weekly Summary

Daytona Speedweeks

Odds for Daytona Clash - February 9 7:00 PM - FS1

The wait for the 2021 season is finally over, and a lot has changed during the offseason. To get started lets talk about the upcoming week and how Daytona Speedweeks has changed from what it previosuly was. The 2021 season kicks off with the Busch Clash on the Daytona Road Coarse (new to the 2021 schedule). There will be no practice or qualifying for the Clash so expect to see similar performance from last years race. Qualifying will be a random draw (similar to last year) that will be broadcasted on Monday night on DIS Facebook.

Best Bets

My eyes are on Chase Elliott (+175) and Martin Truex Jr. (+300) for the Clash. The odds are obviosuly in their favor by a ton compared to the feild (a little too much), and for that reason it leaves us with some solid value bets.

Value Bets

For value, my eyes are on Denny Hamlin (+800) and Kyle Busch (+1600). +800 is really good value for a guy who finished 2nd in the last race here and Denny Hamlin is a sweat in exhibition races. I don't have to explain why KB at +1600 isn't a good bet. Kyle Busch is a solid RC racer with plenty of experience on this configuration (Rolex 24), making him one of my favorite bets of the race.

Underdog Bets

For underdog fullsend bets, I like Chris Buescher (+10000) and Austin Dillon (+10000). Both extremely undervalued and should be in the +2500 area in my opinion. Austin Dillon comes to this race with the most recent experience (Rolex 24 a week ago) and capable RCR equipment. Chris Buescher is a great sleeper bet, after finishing a RC career high at this track last year (4th), +10000 is value city.
